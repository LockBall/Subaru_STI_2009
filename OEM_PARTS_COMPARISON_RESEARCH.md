Last reviewed: 2026-07-16.

- [Engine air filter](AIR_FILTER_RESEARCH.md)

## Evidence standard

Rank evidence in this order:

1. Subaru or manufacturer technical documents.
2. Standardized, attributable test reports.
3. Established catalog specifications.
4. Independent tests with a transparent method.
5. Forum posts, videos, and retailer claims.

Use community evidence when it shows the exact part, vehicle, method, and measurements. Do not call an alternative “better” without comparable evidence.
