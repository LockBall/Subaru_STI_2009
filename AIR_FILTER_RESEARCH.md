# Engine Air Filter — 2009 WRX STI (EJ257)

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16. Scope: stock airbox. This is the canonical air-filter document.

## Recommendation

- **Buy:** aFe POWER Pro DRY S `31-10161`.
- **Disposable alternative:** WIX `49012`.
- **Best-documented disposable alternative:** K&N Advantage `KNA-2304`.
- **Dry disposable with stated 24,000-mile interval:** K&N `KNX-2304`.

aFe is the best-supported option: it is oil-free, directly listed for 2008–2014 STI applications, and has a published 99.2% ISO-coarse-dust efficiency claim. It is washable, so it needs periodic cleaning but no oil. Frame and seal construction are recorded below, but are not used as a quality ranking.

WIX remains a credible disposable option. WIX’s regional catalog describes synthetic media and a polyurethane seal; current North American reseller listings describe cellulose media and plastisol seals. Record that conflict as unresolved. Product photos are useful for checking shape and fit, but cannot reliably identify seal material. A felt seal would not by itself be a reason to reject a filter.

Oiled filters are excluded.

## Buying checks

1. Use an authorized retailer and select **2009 Subaru Impreza WRX STI 2.5 L**—not a generic Impreza listing.
2. Compare the new element with the old one before installation: footprint, thickness, locating shape, and perimeter seal.
3. Clean loose debris from the lower airbox and ensure every airbox latch closes fully.
